What You Will Learn as an Air Ticketing Intern

An air ticketing internship is designed to take you from absolute beginner to a confident ticketing professional. Here is what a typical internship covers:

  • GDS Systems: Hands-on training on Amadeus or Galileo — flight searches, fare displays, PNR creation, ticketing, and cancellations
  • Fare Construction: Understanding fare basis codes, booking classes, taxes, service fees, and how to calculate total ticket costs
  • Airline Knowledge: Learning about different airline types (full-service, low-cost, hybrid), their policies, and baggage rules
  • Geography and Routing: Understanding world geography, major airline hubs, and how to plan optimal travel routes
  • Client Communication: Handling phone calls and emails, responding to travel enquiries, and providing booking confirmations
  • Documentation: Maintaining booking records, issuing invoices, and coordinating with accounts for payment processing

Skills You Need to Succeed

You do not need prior travel experience to apply for an air ticketing internship. However, these qualities will help you succeed:

  • Strong communication skills — You will speak with clients over the phone and via email daily. Clear and professional communication is essential.
  • Basic computer proficiency — Comfort with MS Office, email, and typing. You will learn GDS software during the internship.
  • Attention to detail — Ticketing involves names, dates, flight numbers, and fare calculations. Accuracy is critical.
  • Willingness to learn — The travel industry evolves constantly. Interns who ask questions and take notes learn faster.
  • Basic geography knowledge — Knowing countries, capitals, and major airports will help you settle into the role more quickly.

Internship vs. Direct Full-Time: Which Is Right for You?

If you are unsure whether to apply for an internship or go directly for a full-time role, here is a simple guide:

  • Choose the internship if you are still in college, have no prior exposure to travel systems, or want to test the industry with a low-pressure commitment.
  • Choose full-time if you have completed your education, need financial stability, or already have basic GDS knowledge and want to start building your career immediately.

BTW Group offers both options. If you are unsure, start with the internship — it carries no long-term obligation and often leads to a full-time offer.

FAQs About Air Ticketing Internships in Thane and Navi Mumbai

What does an air ticketing intern do?

An air ticketing intern learns to use GDS systems (Amadeus, Galileo) to search flights, check fares, issue tickets, handle cancellations, and assist clients with travel bookings. Interns work under the guidance of senior ticketing executives and receive structured training.

Do I need experience to apply for an air ticketing internship in Thane?

No. Air ticketing internships are designed for freshers with 0 years of experience. Employers look for good communication skills, basic computer knowledge, and a willingness to learn. No prior GDS or travel experience is required.

What is the stipend for an air ticketing intern in Thane and Navi Mumbai?

Air ticketing intern stipends in Thane and Navi Mumbai typically range from INR 5,000 to INR 12,000 per month. Some internships also offer performance-based incentives. Full-time entry-level roles after internship pay significantly higher.

What skills do I need to become an air ticketing intern?

Key skills include good verbal and written communication, basic computer proficiency, a willingness to learn GDS systems, attention to detail, and basic knowledge of world geography. A GDS certification is a plus but not mandatory.

How long does an air ticketing internship last?

Most air ticketing internships last 3 to 6 months. Some companies offer flexible durations based on academic requirements. High-performing interns may receive a pre-placement offer (PPO) for a full-time role before the internship ends.

Can I apply for an air ticketing internship while still in college?

Yes. Many internships accept college students, provided they can commit to a minimum number of hours per week. Part-time internships or those with flexible schedules are available at some travel agencies in Thane and Navi Mumbai.
