What Is an Insurance Executive at BTW Financial Services?

An Insurance Executive at BTW Financial Services is a salaried, office-based professional responsible for the operational aspects of insurance services. You will work from our Thane or Mumbai office, handling policy processing, documentation, client servicing, and coordination with insurance providers. This is a structured desk role ideal for freshers who want to build a career in the insurance sector with a fixed monthly income, regular working hours, and employee benefits.

Key Responsibilities

  • Processing insurance policy applications — travel insurance, health insurance, and other coverage products
  • Calculating premiums and providing accurate quotes to clients
  • Managing policy documentation, renewals, amendments, and cancellations
  • Assisting clients with claim filing, documentation, and status tracking
  • Coordinating with insurance providers for policy issuance and claim processing
  • Maintaining accurate client records and policy databases
  • Responding to client enquiries via phone and email about policy coverage, benefits, and claims
  • Supporting the sales team with policy-related documentation and compliance
